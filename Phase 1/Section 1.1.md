Assignment Title: Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1
Prompt: Create an image of  large-scale mural on a city wall using acrylic paint, designed to protest against Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). This mural should be placed in the downtown of a city where it can inspire others to protest against ICE. The main message is to let others know to continue to speak up for they believe is not only right, but equal. This mural will contain hard working hispanic citizens sitting on lawn chairs outside while drinking Jaritos as ice cubes melt under their chairs. This style should be meaningful and energetic with vibrant colors. 
Vocabulary Breakdown: The medium/ mediums are the materials or tools used by an artist to create a piece of art. The meaning in artwork is the main message or theme that the artist potrays in the work. The subject matter is the "what" in the art. The subject matter in my art piece is to protest against Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). Representational Art is a style of art that clearly represents real world situations.
